The Law - Data Protection Act 1998

Weycrest.Com Limited will only contact you in order to resolve operational or billing matters. We will never divulge any of your details unless we have to under the Data Protection Act. Weycrest do not send random marketing emails to personal email addresses (spam) and also conform to the requirements of the Data Protection Act, 1998

We will ensure that all UK statutory requirements are complied with. We will adhere to the data protection principles contained within the Data Protection Act 1998, and monitor our internal processes to ensure compliance.

We will pass information on to the necessary legal authorities when requested through the necessary legal framework (court orders) or where we believe the customer is engaged in illegal activity using our services.

All data will only be stored on databases located in the UK, and thus fall under the jurisdiction of UK Law.

Information Collection and Use

Weycrest Solutions Limited is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Weycrest Solutions Limited collects information from our users at several different points on our website.

Order

We request information from the user on our registration, order and payment forms. Here a user must provide contact information (like name and address) and financial information (like credit card number, expiration date). This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill customer's orders. If we have trouble processing an order, this contact information is used to get in touch with the user.

Credit Cards and Payments

We maintain your credit card details in a secure, encrypted format on our system for recurring billing. Your credit card and other personal details will only be passed to Paypal, 2Checkout or Natwest (Streamline) through their secure payment gateway for payment processing.

All recurring billing will be advised by email to the account administration email address prior to the next billing period. Failure to process the billing on the renewal date for the service will lead to the service being suspended until the payment has been successfully processed.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site and is encrypted so cannot be read by any other website. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. It will also terminate once a period of time has elapsed since first using the site.

We only use cookies for your convenience; for instance, to prevent the user from having to re-enter shopping cart information. If a user rejects the cookie, ordering functionality for the user will be affected.

Raw Log Files and Webalizer Stats

We use IP addresses to analyse trends, administer the site, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Links

This web site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we [Weycrest Solutions Limited] are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site.
