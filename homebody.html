What is Web Hosting?

Web Hosting is a service allowing you to publish your website on the internet. Once your website is built, you can upload it to our web hosting service, hosted on our servers. Then anyone in the world can visit your site.

Website hosting allows you space on our server. Once files (images, video's) are uploaded, they are available immediately for anyone to view.

Weycrest hosting packages are designed for all types of web sites. Our servers are connected to the internet 24/7, over multiple, high capacity links. We also monitor the servers around-the-clock, so your sites are always online.

What can Cheap Web Hosting be used for?

Pretty much anything, where you need to publish content over the internet. The content can include image and video files. With web hosting packages there are not particularly limits provided you keep within our terms of conditions.

Many customers use our hosting service for hobby or personal websites. Needless to say our hosting packages are great if you're running an online business require a website for it.

Blog and forum's are increasingly popular uses for affordable web hosting. These sites usually require database and and php scripting in order that blogging software such as Wordpress and phpBB work correctly.
