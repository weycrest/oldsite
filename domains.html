Free Web Forwarding

Many dialup or broadband ISP's, usually provide free webspace. Our free web forwarding service redirects requests to your domain to your ISP free web space and visitors with no annoying popups, banners or adverts which will deter customers and is guaranteed destroy any professional online presence. Web forwarding is a very useful system but can be a drawback when it comes to search engine optimisation (known as SEO). Many search engines cannot catalogue forwarded sites correctly. For those customers wishing to establish a respectable page rank and therefore larger amounts of web traffic Weycrest recommend a Hosting Solution.

Free Email Forwarding

Email forwarding is a free facility allowing you to have unlimited email addresses for your domain. For example sales@yourdomain.co.uk , info@yourdomain.co.uk etc. All mail received at these addresses will be forwarded automatically to your existing email address, or Hotmail account. Forwarding personalises your email address and gives a more professional appearance. For example admin@yourdomain.com looks much better than user@xyz392.freeserve.co.uk. Also your domain address is portable an independent of your ISP. If you change your dialup and ADSL provider, just repoint your forwarding to the new address!
