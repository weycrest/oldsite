Thu Sep 21 19:57:23 BST 2006 - Serious Outage - Redbus Network

We have just experienced a serious network outage (approx 90 minutes downtime) on our Redbus Sovereign House Network, caused by problems with the Cisco BGP router. Network staff are still investigating but it is thought it might due to some kind of DoS attack. This would only have caused problems for customers on or mercury server (also our signup server secure1 would have been unavailable).

We have decided to publish our network uptimes. A temporary status link can be found at www.paullee.eu. Additional servers are being added daily to the Alertra monitoring.

Wed Aug 9 13:28:20 BST 2006 - Weycrest expands into Coreix

Following recent space and power restrictions imposed by Redbus Interhouse, Weycrest has started colocating within Coreix. Unlike Redbus which is "only down the road" from us on the Isle of Dogs, Coreix is based in the Stratford in E15, within the VSNL facility which is still very local.

Coreix boasts good support - genuine 24/7 response and excellent infrastucture. Their Network operates in a 14,000 sq.ft raised floored state-of-the-art data centre which offers complete redundancy in protected power, HVAC, fire suppression, network connectivity and on-site manned security.

Coreix's core network architecture utilises high quality equipment from Juniper, Foundry and Cisco, which is setup in a fully redundant mesh it allows us to offer high availability and exceptional throughput to our end users.

Whilst Weycrest will be taking Coreix/Dedimax transit in the short term (we are impressed with the speed and resilience of their network) we are not ruling out setting up our own fibre interconnects from Telehouse North.

Last week we housed a test server on a new HP Proliant Server. Its likely Coreix will house our Virtuozzo VPS hosting stack, and give us the ability to expand our dedicated server and colocations offerings.
