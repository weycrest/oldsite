Website Promotion

Many people ask us if we have a "web submission service" like other hosting companies. The simple answer is no, as it's just a good way of charging for a service which achieves very little.

The only increase in traffic you will experience after using a web submission service is spam traffic. The best way to promote your site is to write standard compliant websites with plenty of content and get other highly ranked sites to link to yours. That way the search engines find you and it is often quicker than manual submission. This can be done by "link exchanges." At first it can be hard to get established and `authoratitive` sites to link to yours. The solution is purchase text links from busy established sites. The links below should help you build a more "search engine friendly" site and promote it.

  • SEO Chat
  • Search Engine Watch

But be careful who you link to and from. Linking from a "link farm" or an adult site will probably do more harm and good. Try and select "quality" sites to link to yours. Install Google Toolbar and check out the Page Rank of sites you would like to have a link on. Look for a PR of 6 or higher. Choice of the anchor link is important which should incorporate your keywords. Don't use your domain or site name unless it includes your "keywords."

A keyword popularity tool is helpful in building up a bank of useful keywords for the trade or service you are engaged in. Don't "spam" your website by throwing in lots of the same keywords. This could earn you a search engine ban. Include keywords into the body of your text in a natural way. Importantly provide good useful content. If you are selling second hand bikes, don't just put up an order form. Include sections on bike maintenance and safety. You could also include a section on your site giving details of great bike rides.
