Trinity Buoy Server Housing

We can only host rack optimised equipment in Redbus. For larger equipment or servers using desktop cases we can accomodate for a standard charge of £30 per month to include a UPS port. We currently have a 2Mbit/s microwave link between Trinity Buoy and Redbus Sovereign House (shortly to be upgraded to 10 Mbit/s). Contact us for further details.

Payment Terms

Payment is on a quarterly basis, so we would initially require three months in advance. Customers paying annually will receive an additional discount of equivalent to one months rack rental.

Additional Rack Space

Rack space can be increase for an additional £15 per additional 'U'. Each additional 'U' entitles you to 0.25A more power and a further switch port. If you need more than a few 'U' extra, please get in touch and we'll calculate a custom price, based on likely power and switch useage. The price to co-locate one 4U server will be lower than 4 x 1U servers.

Nagios Server Monitoring

£10 per month per server and all common port ie HTTP, SMTP, POP, DNS, FTP etc, with a one-off setup charge of £10. Setup is waived if ordered at thetime as the colocation installation.

Free Technical Support

Basic support for free. This includes basic support queries and occasional reboots during office hours. Hopefully clients are reasonably self-sufficient. However, should you require a greater level of support on an on-going basis. We can quote for a package with guaranteed response times etc.

Server Management (advanced technical support)

Includes system maintenance, patching or upgrading requiring one of our Linux technically-trained staff to attend your server (either accompanied, or on a remote-hands basis) is charged at the following rates:

  • Office Hours - £45 per callout, including the first 60 minutes. Each additional 60 minutes is charged at £35.
  • Outside of the hours - £95 for the callout and first 60 minutes, and £75 for each additional 60 minutes.

Office hours are 9am-5pm GMT Mon-Fri (excluding bank holidays). Within the above rates, we are able to perform more complex tasks such as the installation of software, configuration of your server and more advanced troubleshooting.
