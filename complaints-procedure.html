Complaints Escalation Procedure

Hosted is committed to providing an outstanding level of service to its customers.

As part of this service the Customer Escalation Procedure provides a mechanism by which customers who feel that they have been let down or treated unfairly can escalate their problem within Hosted. All complaints are reviewed by a senior manager.

First Level Escalation

Compose an email defining your complaint and send it to escalation1 [ at ] weycrest.com

Your complaint will be logged, assigned and investigated. Your complaint will be reviewed in our weekly service delivery meeting. We will respond to your email within 5 working days telling you how we intend to resolve your complaint.

Second Level Escalation

Compose an email defining your complaint and attaching the first level escalation information. Send it to escalation2 [ at ] weycrest.com.

Your complaint will be logged and assigned to the director responsible for customer services. Your complaint will be reviewed in our weekly management meeting. We will respond to your email within 5 working days.

Legal Redress

If you remain unsatisfied with our attempts to resolve your issue you can contact Otelo, the Office of the Telecommunications Ombudsman. Their web site is at www.otelo.org.uk and email address is enquiries@otelo.org.uk.
