Web Hosting Help

Often there are a lots of confusing terms, splashed aroung on web hosting site. This short guide will attempt to explain what they mean!

Plesk Control Panel - Plesk is the Control Panel used to maintain and administer your website and email. Click here for the Plesk Control Panel Tour

Multi Domain - The ability to host more than one distinct web site in your hosting space. The number is shown in "Number of Websites." Also in Plesk you can "brand" your control panel and permit "Domain Administrator" to individual domains, so effectively Plesk Plus, Plesk Pro and Plesk Ultimate packages are mini reseller plans.

Data Transfer - Sometimes called `Bandwidth` is how much data you can transfer to and from your web hosting account within a monthly period. This includes all data including email, uploading and download your web files and images, clicks to your website.

Disk Space Sometimes called `Storage` - This is the physical amount of data that can be held on your web hosting space, mainly website files, photos, but also databases, emails and log files.

Subdomain - A sub domain allows you to host multiple websites off of your main web domain, for example: forum.yourdomain.com or store.yourdomain.com.

Domain Aliases - A Domain Alias allows you to point hundreds of other domains to your main hosted domain. So that yourdomain.co.uk could resolves to yourhosteddomain.com

MySQL databases - MySQL is a type of database, which powers many dynamic websites, such as blogs, forums and ecommerce. All the data is held in the MySQL database and scripting language such as PHP or Perl, will pull out this data and display it in your webspace.

Scheduled Tasks - Otherwise know as "Cron Jobs" to those familiar with Unix parlance. Basically the ability to run a script a scheduled times

Mod Rewrite - An advanced feature of Apache Web Server is the ability to rewrite URL's. Why would you want to do this? Many dynamic sites such as Joomla and Wordpress create complex, human and seo unfriendly URL's out of the box. Mod Rewrite provides the ability to rewrite these into a more memorable and search engine friendly format, and is controllable normally from a .htaccess file, uploaded to your web space.

Password Protected Directories - The ability to password protect a directory or directories on you webspace, and only allow authorized users to access.
