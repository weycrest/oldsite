True Multi Domain Hosting

Reseller Multi Domain Hosting with "Web Host Manager" (or WHM) as the account control panel. WHM is where you create your customer accounts. After entering some account information just click "create." This will instantly setup the domain and create a personal "cPanel" control panel for your users. All you need to do then is set the nameservers for your domain at your registrar. Once logged into their individual cPanel your customers can then create their own subdomains, email addresses, change passwords, and much more without any intervention from you or us.
