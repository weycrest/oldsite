How to transfer your Domain Name from 1and1 (1&1 Internet)

At Weycrest, we manage quite a few domain name. You can be sure your domain is in the right place.

Just follow the instructions below to move your domain to Weycrest

For .uk Domain Names

Goto http://contract.1and1.co.uk

  • Log in using your account number and password
  • Choose the contract which contains the domains
  • Select whether you want to "Cancel Entire Contract" or "Cancel Selected Domains"
  • Choose the "Execute On" for the domain
  • Change the cancellation option to "Change Provider"
  • Click "Continue"
  • Enter our IPS tag: WEYCREST
  • Click "Finish"
  • "1and1" will should you an email to confirm you are the owner and complete the final transfer
