ATX or Tower Colocation

One advantage of using Coreix is that they can provide ATX or Tower Server Colocation on their "breadracks." Its no longer mentioned on their website, but give their sales/support a call anywau. Mounting non rack optimised kit would be difficult or prohibitively expensive in any other data centre such as Redbus. Tower servers generally have better cooling than rack optimized equipment (as well as being cheaper of course).

Redbus Harbour Exchange

When capacity is available again, your server will be housed in our secure data rack at our London Data Centre (Harbour Exchange). We don't own this data centre but rent these facilities from Redbus on the eighth floor of Harbour Exchange. All Redbus datacentre's boast redundant power supplies, redundant network feeds, FM200 fire suppression, redundant air conditioning - all the industry standard data centre facilities. All servers are connected to our network via a 100 Mbit/s port switch. We have an excellent multihomed connection supplied via a number of Tier One providers.

Trinity Buoy Server Housing

Larger equipment or servers using desktop or tower cases we cannot be accomodated at Redbus, but this equipment can be accomodated on shelving at our Trinity Buoy Wharf NOC for a standard charge of £20 per month to include a UPS port and 10GB per month of transfer.

Due to excellent line of site across the River Thames we currently have a secure 2Mbit/s microwave link between Trinity Buoy and Redbus Sovereign House (shortly to be upgraded to 10 Mbit/s). Reliability of the wireless link is excellent and is comparable with a leased line. The radio link provider (awellconnected.com) is supplying a service better than the SLA agreed figure. During 2005 we intend to add a backup radio link or landline in addition to our current wireless and ADSL connectivity.

This network is especially useful for providing backup or tertiary mail or DNS services as it exists on a different subnet to our main networks and is sufficiently fast to enable some overnight "off site" backups to be carried out. Indeed the link was originally financed and setup for Simply.Com in order to backup critical DNS and domain forwarding information.

Trinity Buoy is located a few moments car drive from Telehouse or Global Switch in East India Dock. The Redbus data centres are slightly further away on the Isle of Dogs peninsular but only about 5 minutes drive away depending on prevailing traffic conditions as the area is close to the Blackwall Tunnel. There is parking at Trinity Buoy and we currently have spare studio or "deskspace" with excellent views of the Millenium Dome and the River Thames for the accomodation of NOC engineers, server technicians or web designers. These facilities can be hired on an hourly, daily or weekly basis. Contact us for further details and availability
