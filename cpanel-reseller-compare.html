Does "Unlimited" Hosting Mean Unlimited?

Question: "Do you really offer unlimited traffic and web space?"

Answer: You can host as many websites you like, as there is no Cpanel licencing restriction, provided they do not, in total, use more than 10% of the server's processing power. This means some customers can run hundreds of static sites. Obviously if the sites are dynamic, or you require lots of email accounts, then you will not be able to host as many web sites.

Question: "What happens if my sites use more than 10% of the servers resources"

Answer: We are able to accomodate some temporary "burst" to the normal allowed 10% however we will be happy to discuss with you alternative solutions for your hosting requirements, such as a dedicated server.

Additionally we do not allow the following types of hosting or activies on our facilities:

  • Pure adult orientated websites (erotic, artistic or photographic may be OK) - please check if unsure.
  • File distribution websites e.g. a website distributing very large movies, software or mp3, includes Bittorrent and other P2P networks
  • Hotlinking or "Leeching"Hosting, graphics, banners, animations or cgi/php/python scripts or databases for other websites hosted elsewhere
  • Backup, Storage, File or FTP Hosting Storing pages, files, data or executable as a repository for other websites, servers or desktops machines
  • Secondary reselling Not to offer reseller hosting space to third parties, or give away web space under a domain, sub domain or directory

Files stored on your account must also be hyperlinked or related to the websites hosted in your reseller space. Additionally our hosting is not suitable for mass emailing systems (which ideally need a dedicated server or VPS). For guidelines on mail usage Click Here
